Dependency injection - template

Demonstration of dependency injection mechanism into template
This template, and all injected templates and modules are loaded at the same time asynchronously.

View location: 

/app/views/dynamic-data/di/template.html

injected template.html: ${this.template.injected[0]({ heading: "heading of injected template.html", body: "body of injected template.html", })}
injected custom-module1: ${this.template.injected[1].getHtmlContent( "heading of injected custom-module1", "body of injected custom-module1", )}

${this.template.injected[2].sayHiToConsole()}
